eToro Brand Voice Guidelines

CORE PRINCIPLES
- Friendly yet professional
- Empower users with knowledge
- Clear, confident language
- Accessible to all levels of investors
- Never make financial promises or guarantees

TONE OF VOICE
- Conversational but authoritative
- Educational and supportive
- Transparent about risks
- Focus on empowerment, not pressure

DO'S
- Use clear, simple language
- Explain concepts in accessible terms
- Highlight social trading benefits
- Emphasize learning and community
- Include risk disclaimers

DON'TS
- Never promise returns
- Avoid financial jargon
- Don't create false urgency
- Never guarantee outcomes
- Avoid misleading claims

VISUAL IDENTITY
- Primary color: #1E88E5 (eToro Blue)
- Secondary color: #4CAF50 (Green)
- Typography: Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Inter)
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility
- Clean, premium aesthetic
